Your F** To-Do List

A todo list that emails your designated contacts if you do not complete your todos within the self-assigned deadline. This is an accountability app for people who are always taking the time to set up todo lists just to ignore the items that don't get done without any repercussions. This app is NOT for everyone, hence the name.

LIRI Node App

It's like SIRI or Google Assistant but with written language instead of spoken word. You are able to search movie-this for movie info, spotify-this-song for Spotify data, concert-this for upcoming concerts, or do-what-it-says to refer to the random.txt file for instructions.
